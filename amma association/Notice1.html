Upcoming National Conference on Bio pesticide Restriction in India (New Delhi)

National Conference on Bio-pesticide registration in India

Date- Postpond due to COVID-19 outbreak
Venue- Taj Vivanta, Dwarka, New Delhi

AMMA Association extends our immense pleasure and honor in extending you a warm invitation to be a part of the National Conference on Bio-pesticide registration in India as an “ Ambassador” to make the event successful. The conference is going to be held on 15th Mar 2020 at Hotel Taj Vivanta, Dwarka, New Delhi.The theme of the summit is to register Bio-pesticides in India and know the upcoming challenges faced by stakeholders.

Objectives:-

.  To make a platform of registration of Bio-pesticides in India

· To know the facts and problems of Bio-pesticides registration

· To bring the stakeholders under one banner

· To share the data for registration to stakeholders

· New upcoming research in the field of biocontrol agents

· Status of PGPR product challenges in registration

The conference would cater to a round table and panel discussion, invited talks. The professional will be stakeholders of AMMA association, Central Insecticide Board officials, Government authorities, Agriculture ministry, Bio-pesticide and agriculture input manufacturers, Business operators, Scientists, Technocrats, Experts, Exporters from all over India.

Registration fee -

AMMA association data members – Rs. 3000 / person

Non-members Company – Rs. 5500 / Accompanying person – Rs. 3000

Scientist/ technocrat / research worker – Rs. 4000 / person

Government authorities – Rs. 4000 / person
